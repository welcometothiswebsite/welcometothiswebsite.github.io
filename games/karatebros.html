FAQ

Why isn't the game loading?

You should check your internet connection and make sure our site isn't blocked. Also, disable all ad blockers.

How do I play?

Just hop in and start suplexing your bros!

Which browser should I use?

You should use chrome. It is the only browser guaranteed to work. Karate Bros has also been tested to work on FireFox and Safari, but not guaranteed.

Are there more Bros games?

Yes! Our newest is Football Bros which is still in development. But we also have Soccer Bros and Basket Bros.
